Our Pizza Story

From a small oven in Athens to the fastest pizza delivery in Greece

Welcome to ToMyPizza!

Founded in 1995 by Tony Romano, ToMyPizza! started as a small family-owned pizzeria in Athens with one simple mission: deliver the freshest, hottest pizza possible. Tony noticed that pizza always tasted best right out of the oven, so he created a revolutionary delivery system specifically for the streets of Athens.

Today, we continue that legacy with our proprietary "Hot-Now" delivery system that ensures your pizza arrives at your door still bubbling hot from our ovens, whether you're in Aghia Paraskevi, Chalandri, or anywhere in Athens.

Our Mission in Greece

To deliver authentic, restaurant-quality pizza at home, with the same temperature and freshness as if you were eating at our Athens pizzeria, combining traditional Italian techniques with Greek ingredients.
